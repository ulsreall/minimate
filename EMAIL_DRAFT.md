### What is MiniMate?

MiniMate replaces complex DeFi interfaces with a simple chat experience. MiniPay users can:

- **Check balances** — CELO, USDm, USDC, USDT in real-time
- **Track spending** — Auto-categorized breakdown from on-chain transactions
- **Set savings goals** — Visual progress tracking toward targets
- **Send payments** — Step-by-step flow: amount → token → address → confirm

### Why it matters for MiniPay users

- **No CELO needed** — Gas is paid in USDm via fee abstraction (`feeCurrency` parameter)
- **Natural language** — "Send 10 USDm to mom" instead of copy-pasting 0x addresses
- **Real data** — Not mock. Uses Blockscout API for actual transaction history
- **Auto-connects** — Detects MiniPay wallet instantly, no "Connect" button needed
- **Mobile-first** — Built for the 10M+ users in emerging markets who use MiniPay daily
