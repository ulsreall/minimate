Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your wallet, we receive your public address. This is stored in your browser session only.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send to MiniMate are processed to generate responses. They are not stored on our servers after the session ends.
  • Savings Goals: Goals you create are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We never see this data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Private keys, seed phrases, or mnemonics
  • Personal identification (name, email, phone)
  • IP addresses or device fingerprints
  • Browsing history or cookies for tracking

3. Third-Party Services

  • Celo Blockchain: All transactions are public on-chain. Celoscan

4. On-Chain Data

Blockchain transactions are public and permanent. Your wallet address and transaction history are visible to anyone. MiniMate reads this data to provide spending analysis.
